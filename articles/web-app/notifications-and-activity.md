# Notifications & Activity

DotEnv keeps you informed two ways: an **activity log** that records what happened in your
organization, and **notifications** for things that need your attention.

## Activity log

The activity log is your organization's audit trail. Open it at **Activity**
(`/dashboard/activity`).

It records actions taken across the organization — for example creating or changing projects,
secrets, members, API keys, and settings — so you can see **who did what and when**. Use it to:

- Review recent changes and confirm an action took place.
- Investigate unexpected changes.
- Support compliance and audits.

> Availability and retention of detailed audit logs can depend on your plan. See
> [Billing & Plans](billing-and-plans).

## Notifications

Notifications surface important events directly in the dashboard. Open **Notifications**
(`/dashboard/notifications`) to see your list, and open any item
(`/dashboard/notifications/{notification}`) to read the full details.

Use this area to catch up on things like invitations, billing or usage warnings, and other
account or organization events.

## Email preferences

You control which notifications also reach you by **email** from your account's
**Notifications** settings (`/account/settings/notifications`). Adjust these preferences to opt in
or out of specific categories so you only get the emails you want.

> Tip: keep billing and security-related notifications enabled so you don't miss usage warnings,
> grace-period notices, or sign-in alerts.
